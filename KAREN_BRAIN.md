# KAREN BRAIN — The Decision Engine

> KAREN BRAIN is not an agent.  
> It is the strategic mind above agents.  
> It combines instinct built from 17 years of real decisions with Claude's cold logic.

---

## What It Is

KAREN BRAIN is a **dual-engine decision system** built on Karen Tonoyan's ALFA philosophy:

- **Karen Layer** — 9 life filters developed across 309 real human and business cases
- **Claude Layer** — cold analysis, risk mapping, scenarios, numbers
- **Heart Filter** — absolute override. If the heart says NO, analysis stops immediately.

It does not ask "what should I do?"  
It asks "**should this decision exist at all?**"

---

## Core Mechanisms

| Mechanism | What it does | Why it's unique |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Heart Filter** | First and highest priority check | No logic can override instinct |
| **Line of Resistance** | Hard NEVER / ALWAYS rules — BLOCK, not HOLD | Eliminates grey zones |
| **9 Life Filters** | Analysis → Shortcut → Cost, Pain tolerance, Chaos as laboratory... | Real life wisdom, not prompt engineering |
| **Conflict Detection** | Shows exact point of dispute + resolving test | Makes tension between instinct and logic visible |
| **Decision Trace** | Full JSON audit of every decision | Every verdict is recordable and verifiable |

---

## When It Activates

Automatically on any strategic decision:
- Investment or partnership evaluation
- Hiring or trust assessment
- Build vs Buy
- Pricing or product direction
- "Can I trust this person or system?"

**Manual activation**:
```
/karen "Should I take this deal?"
/karen "Build or buy this component?"
/karen "Can I trust this person with access?"
```

---

## Output Structure

Every KAREN BRAIN analysis produces:

```
HEART STATUS: YES / NO / NOT YET
LINIA OPORU: CLEAR / VIOLATED: [reason]

KAREN (Instinct Layer)     — gut check through 9 filters
CLAUDE (Logic Layer)       — cold analysis, risk, numbers

CONFLICT DETECTED: YES / NO
  → Point of dispute
  → Risk if you follow Karen
  → Risk if you follow Claude
  → Resolving test

FINAL VERDICT: PASS / HOLD / BLOCK
```

If Heart says NO → output is immediate:  
*"Stop. Don't waste time on analysis. Keep searching."*

If Line of Resistance is violated → output is BLOCKED before analysis begins.
